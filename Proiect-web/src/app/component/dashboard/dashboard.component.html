Tic-Tac-Toe dates back to ancient times!

Did you know that Tic-Tac-Toe has a rich history that can be traced back thousands of years? This game, also known as noughts and crosses, is believed to have originated in ancient Egypt. Archaeologists have discovered drawings resembling Tic-Tac-Toe on the walls of Egyptian temples, dating as far back as 1300 BCE.
